How this works

This site is shared. Everyone with the link sees and edits the same live data — no separate copies, no spreadsheet uploads needed for viewers.

The weekly routine:

  1. In Clover, export your inventory (the multi-tab Excel file).
  2. Click "Upload export." It updates stock for everyone and compares to last week.
  3. Check the weekly summary and the "Needs reorder" tab.

Logging deliveries: when stock arrives, click "＋ Add" on the item (or "Add a delivery" up top for many at once). Enter packages, units per package, and loose units — it does the math. The pack size is remembered as that item's default but can be changed any time.

Backups: data lives in cloud storage and is reliable, but "Download backup" gives you a safety file you can keep in Google Drive just in case.
